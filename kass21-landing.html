Her Journey

Built Through Every Chapter

2023 · The Beginning

Adversity First. Championship Next.

Kassidy's first competitive season wasn't easy. She entered girls softball with a coach who sidelined her and six other players in favor of coaches' kids. She made plays from left field when the league president was watching and earned the only nickname that mattered — Clutch Cargill. After resigning from that team, she was invited mid-season onto Romo's Arsenal boys baseball squad by the league president himself. She started at pitcher in her first game, made the first two outs, hit every at-bat, and ran the bases like she'd been doing it her whole life. Arsenal swept everything: opening tournament, regular season #1, end-of-season tournament. Triple champions in her first year.

2024 · Two Leagues, One Vision

The Bananas Didn't Give Up Asking.

After the Arsenal championship season, Coach Brian of the Bananas reached out — and on that same day, so did Coach Romo with a D1 kid-pitch baseball invite. The family chose one more season with Arsenal for loyalty. When Coach Brian called a second time — this time while she was mid-commitment with a D1 10U boys baseball team — she said yes to both. She honored every commitment to the baseball team while transitioning over, and in Spring 2024 posted .548 AVG and 1.259 OPS. She hadn't adjusted. She'd arrived.

2025–2026 · Hotshots Era

Silent Leader. Maximum Effort. Every Time.

Three seasons with Hotshots. No drama, no shortcuts. Spring 2025 was her offensive peak — .426 AVG, .806 SLG, 6 home runs, 45 stolen bases. Kassidy settled primarily into 3B, SS, C, and CF, bringing catcher IQ and infield instincts to every spot. She's the kind of player who leads by showing up, working harder than everyone else, and making the person next to her raise their level. Coaches don't have to chase her energy — she brings it.
